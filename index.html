About

Myself

Hi there! I'm Michelle.

I'm in second year of my undergrad at Wilfrid Laurier University, where I'm in the double degree in Business Administration and Computer Science. Currently, I'm looking for an internship in Software Engineering. I love getting my hands dirty and working on multi-disciplinary projects. Throughout my undergrad, I've had the opportunity to work on several awesome projects through extracirriculars, school and hobby. While I lean towards involving myself in the technical side of things, I enjoy taking the lead and helping a team work towards a common goal.


In my free time, I enjoy creating digital art, reading, and running.
